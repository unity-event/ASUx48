A thought-provoking event examining how cinema and television silently rewire our psychology, reshape our cultural identity, and mobilize society at large. Join us for an evening that challenges how you see the screen — and yourself.

What We Explore

Three Dimensions
of Influence

🧠

Psychological Effects

How media influences our thoughts, emotions, and behaviors — from narrative transportation and parasocial relationships to fear conditioning and empathy cultivation through storytelling.

🌐

Cultural Influence

Shaping values, beliefs and global perspectives. How film and television export ideologies, redefine beauty standards, and homogenize or preserve cultural identities across borders.

👥

Social Impact

From raising awareness to shaping public opinion and social change. Media as a catalyst for activism, a mirror of inequality, and a powerful force in political discourse.

What We'll Be
Talking About

TV Series · Drama

13 Reasons Why

⚠ Youth Suicide Rates

Netflix's controversial series depicting teenage suicide in graphic detail sparked a documented rise in youth suicide rates following its release. We examine how irresponsible storytelling can turn fiction into a real-world public health crisis.

Film · Thriller

Jaws

⚠ Tourism & Marine Fear

Spielberg's 1975 blockbuster single-handedly reshaped public perception of the ocean. Beach tourism plummeted, shark hunting surged, and an irrational fear of open water persisted for decades — all from two hours of cinema.
